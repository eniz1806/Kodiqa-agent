## FAQ

**Can I use Kodiqa at work for free?**
Yes — if you're using Kodiqa as a developer tool on your own machine, no license is needed. The AGPL only applies when you modify and distribute Kodiqa or make it available as a service.

**Can I use Kodiqa in my open-source project?**
Yes — as long as your project is also AGPL-3.0 compatible.
